Welcome to Garmin Workout Share extension

How to:

      Share a Workout

       If you are a Garmin user who wants to share a workout with other users, follow these steps:
    1. Go to your Garmin Workouts page. Make sure you are logged in to your account.
    2. Select the workout that you would like to share, and re-open the extension popup window. The "Share Workout" button has become visible.
    3. When you click this button, a file (type .json) will be downloaded to your system. Now you can share your workout by sending this file (via Mail, Facebook, WhatsApp etc.) to your friends who are interested in your workout.
      4.

      Get a Workout

       If you are a Garmin user who wants to get someone else's workout, follow these steps:
    1. Open the "Choose File" dialog at the top of the extension window, and choose the workout file that was sent to you.
    2. The "Get Workout" button has become visible.
    3. When you click this button, you will be redirected to the Garmin website. If you are not logged in to your Garmin account, you will be asked to log in and repeat the process.
    4. If you are logged in, the workout will be saved to your account in a few seconds.
